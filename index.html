About Me

I was born in Srebrenica, Bosnia and Herzegovina in 1995, during the Bosnian War. Despite the difficult circumstances, I persevered and completed my education at the Medical high school in Tuzla. However, I soon realized that my true passion was in the field of information technology.

I decided to pursue my passion and enrolled at the American University in Bosnia and Herzegovina, where I studied information technology. During my time at the university, I gained extensive knowledge and skills in various areas of IT, including programming languages, software development, and database management.

My dedication and hard work paid off, and I graduated with a degree in information technology. Since then, I have been working in the field, gaining valuable experience and honing my skills. I have worked on various projects, including website development, software development, and database management.

I am a highly motivated and detail-oriented professional who is passionate about the field of information technology. I am always eager to learn and stay up-to-date with the latest technologies and trends in the industry. My excellent communication and collaboration skills make me a valuable team member, and my ability to work well under pressure allows me to consistently deliver high-quality work.
